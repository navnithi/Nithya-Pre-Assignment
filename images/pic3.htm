Learning is Fun with RUFUS

Our wise aviator, Rufus echoes our passion for learning and a growth mindset focusing on every child’s uniqueness making learning more joyful while nurturing positive attitude in them.

He is an observant caretaker and ensures to provide safe and hygienic early learning environment.

He encourages communication with fables and rhymes.
He is curious like a child, will take your little one for a joy ride with fun filled activities and will answer all your queries. He takes your munchkin under his strong wings and will ensure that they learn, grow and excel.

Why choose Us?

Nurturing our young learners with 4Cs:
Confidence, Curiosity, Creativity and Compassion

International Curriculum

Crafted by expert educationists from Singapore & India, our curriculum is based on Multiple Intelligence Theory as we empower our young learners and not restrict them to single modality of learning.

Skilled Teachers

Our ECCED qualified teachers are committed to cater the learning needs of young learners and also attend quarterly teachers training program to give your child The Right Start. The Right Path.

Nutritious Meal Plans

We know the importance of balanced meal to meet the nutrition need and energy requirements of growing learners. That’s why we have our meal plans formulated by Nutritionists.

Safety, Security & Hygiene

Child safety defines us, all our staff members are POCSO trained. You can monitor your child anytime through our Live CCTV access. We adhere to the highest quality of hygiene standards at all our centres.
